Software Development Team Roles and Responsibilities

My team is the Single Digital Ecosystem team and as for our responsibilities, we are a full-stack team who help maintaining and developing the gol-ui online groceries website. More specifically, we mainly look after Occasions by Sainsbury's (OBS) page - previously known as Food To Order (FTO) (some still refer to it as this). However, we also support the slot booking page both in home delivery + click & collect, and occasionally, we assist with Favourites page due to some team members having worked on it prior to this team

My Role - Associate Engineer (C3)

I contribute to feature delivery on gol-ui across the tech-stack, with a focus on front-end work (at the moment). Day to day, I pick up tickets from our sprint board, follow our branching methodology, create well-tested code (unit and e2e tests), and raise PRs for review. I pair with seniors to learn patterns and review feedback, help squash bugs, and take part in stand-ups (been a sprint captain), refinement, planning and retros. I also support demos at sprint review and keep documentation (mainly GitHub) up to date when we change behaviour.

Mahdi Joafshani - Senior Software Engineer (C5)

  • Role within the team: Backend Engineer.
  • Main responsibilities: Leads technical approach and sets code quality standards. Breaks down complex work, unblocks others, reviews PRs, and mentors engineers (such as myself).
  • Contributions: Mahdi is a backend developer who works closely with the Architecture and Platform teams. He focuses on making sure our systems are reliable, secure, and run smoothly.

    • Interactions with me: I pair with Mahdi when I want to learn more about the backend side of our team and learn patterns and skills that I can then apply independently.

Louis Oliver - Senior QA Tester (C5)

  • Role within the team: QA Tester.
  • Main responsibilities: Ensures the quality of the product through manual and automated testing. Develops test plans and test cases. Identifies and reports defects, and works with the team to resolve issues.
  • Contributions: Louis makes sure that new features meet the required quality standards. He focuses on creating a seamless user experience by identifying and addressing potential issues before they reach production. He is one of the final security checks before code is merged and deployed.

    • Interactions with me: I work with Louis to confirm that the features I develop meet the quality standards set by the team. He tests my implementations and provides feedback (whether accepted or not), which helps me improve my understanding of testing practices and that I consider quality from the start.

Gabrielle (Gabby) Lipman - Senior Product Manager

  • Role within the team: Product Manager.
  • Main responsibilities: Defines the product vision and strategy. Works with stakeholders to gather requirements and prioritize features. She then collaborates with the team to see to a successful delivery of the product.
  • Contributions: Gabby works closely with the team to ensure that the product meets the needs of the users and the business. She focuses on understanding user needs and translating them into actionable requirements for the team. She really looks out for our team members and advocates for their needs (pushes back stakeholders when necessary).

    • Interactions with me: I communicate with Gabby to ensure that the features I develop align with the product vision and strategy. She provides valuable insights into user needs and helps me prioritize my work effectively. Before my work is deployed, I have to demo my work to Gabby and the team to verify it meets the acceptance criteria and expectations.

Sara Berciano - UX Designer

  • Role within the team: UX Designer.
  • Main responsibilities: Conducts user research and usability testing. Creates wireframes, prototypes, and designs. Collaborates with the team to promise a user-centered design approach.
  • Contributions: Sara checks that the product is user-friendly and meets the needs of the users. She focuses on understanding user behavior and translating that into design solutions. She advocates for the user throughout the design and development process.

    • Interactions with me: I also work closely with Sara to ensure that the features I develop align with the user experience goals. She provides thoughts into user behavior and helps me understand the design needs for the piece of work I am developing on.
